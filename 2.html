CHARTERED ACCOUNTANT


What is a Chartered Accountant?

A chartered accountant (CA) is a financial professional who is qualified to execute certain accounting procedures. It also refers to an accounting designation granted internationally to individuals

Types of Chartered Accountant

1. Finance Manager: Finance Managers are one of the highest-paying jobs after completing a CA course. They have to oversee the financial health of an organization.

2. Accountants: Their main role is to compile and prepare the financial records of a company, budget, and business plans, analyzing and examining the records.

3. Taxation Consultant: A Tax Consultant has to consult their clients in making good decisions about tax-related issues and also help their clients in saving money.

4. CEO: CEOs (Chief Executive Officers) are the topmost job profiles one can become after completing the CA course. Their job role is to contact other companies and the board of directors to help in operating, developing, and managing the company to meet the company’s goal.

5. Auditors: Auditing is a good career one can choose after doing a CA course. Auditors are in charge of reviewing the financial documents for accuracy with laws and regulations.

How to become a Chartered Accountant?

• Undergraduate Level Preparation: Students having an undergraduate degree can also apply for a CA course. They don’t need to sit for the CA CPT Test. Students who are from a commerce background need to score 55% at their undergraduate level. And students who are not from commerce backgrounds and do not have mathematics in their undergraduate degrees are required to score above 60%. Students having mathematics at their Undergraduate level need to score 55% marks to become a CA.

• Post-Graduate Level Preparation: Post-graduate students also can apply for CA courses, they don’t need to seat for CA CPT Exam. However, they must have a minimum aggregate score of 55% and above in Commerce or Mathematics. Students from Science and Arts backgrounds are also eligible to apply for CA courses. They must have a minimum aggregate score of 60% and above in their Master’s degree. Aspiring candidates can register for the course 9 months before the CA IPCC Exam.

CA Courses

Some of the top courses which can be pursued to become a CA are listed below for your reference.

CA Foundation Course

CA Foundation Course is for 4 months. Students need to register for the course after passing 10th exam and after passing 12th standard they are eligible to sit for CA CPT Exam.

• Admission Process: Admission to this CA Foundation course is done through CA CPT Exam. Students need to register for the course, then after passing 12th standard they can appear for CA CPT Exam

• Entrance Exam: CA CPT (Common Proficiency Test) is the entrance exam that must be cleared for studying CA Course from a recognized CA Institute

• Eligibility for Entrance: 12th standard passed out student in Science/ Commerce/ Arts with 55% marks

CA Intermediate Course

CA Intermediate Level course is for 2.5 to 3 years. After passing the CA Foundation course, one can apply for the CA Intermediate Course.

• Admission Process: Admission to this course is done through CA IPCC Exam

• Entrance Exam: Once you clear your CA Foundation course, you will be able to appear for CA IPCC Exam. The total marks of the CA IPCC Exam are 100 marks and out of that you need to score 40% marks

• Eligibility for Entrance: After completing the CA foundation course, you will be able to appear for CA IPCC Exam. Candidates who want to pursue the course after completing UG or PG, can come from the direct entry route method and also must clear the CA Intermediate Examination of ICSI or ICWA Examination

CA Final Course CA Final course is for 2 years. After completing the CA Intermediate course, you can apply for CA Final Course.

• Admission Process: Admission to this course is done after the CA Intermediate level. Students need to secure 40% to study CA Final Course

• Entrance Exam: There is no entrance exam for CA Final Course. One can take admission to this course after completing the 2.5 years article ship and CA Intermediate level course exam
